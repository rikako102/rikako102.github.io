OXO

[June - September 2017]

I was a Product Development Intern for OXO, an innovative consumer products company. I specifically worked with the Cleaning and Organization team. Under that umbrella was household cleaning supplies, bathroom storage, and kitchen organization.

OXO is a titan in the industry for their incredible products that are centered around the user, first and foremost. A wall in their offices is covered floor to ceiling with lost gloves of all kinds - big or small, child or adult, fashion or utility - to remind the employees that we're working to create for everyone, regardless of age, gender, or ability. With this internship, I contributed to OXO's thoughtful process as a problem becomes an idea becomes a product.

My internship's task: get the development process for a future cleaning product up and running.
