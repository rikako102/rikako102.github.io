Spring 2018, I took my first website development class, called Human-Computer Interaction. The skill of creating a better user experience is a common theme in many classes I've taken during my time at Northwestern and I was eager to learn the technical skills to expand my skillset.

My entire website is a passion project, a visual representation of what I have learned. Although it is a relatively simple website, no templates are used. There are four main pages: home, about me, my work, and contact information.
