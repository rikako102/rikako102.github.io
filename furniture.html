The Lagkage
The Lagkage chair is a study of light in white spaces and curvatures. The wooden slats that create the back of the chair are tapered; the shortest is nearest to the seat and gradually gets longer as you get closer to the top. The highest piece fully extends to the front of the chair, creating armrests.
